Equine VR

Equine VR This was the main job I did over the summer of 2022, working at the University developing a VR project. The aim of this project was to produce a prototype AI horse that could respond to 3 parameters in a realistic manner. These were: Focus - Where you are looking and how hard you are looking at it Ask - How much energy you are bringing into yourself Neutral - How you are behaving around the horse These might not be the best definitions, but the more important component is how the horse should respond to these parameters
